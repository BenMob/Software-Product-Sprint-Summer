Hi, I am Ben

I am a rising third year Software Engineering Student at California State University San Marcos. Now, how did I end up in this field ? During my final year of High School, I was indecisve about what I wanted to do. So I decided to take an Introduction to Computer Science course just out of pure curiosity. It was during this time that I began to understand that all the magic about computers were just nothing but very precise set of statements written by some creative human beings somewhere. I found that very fascinating and I knew I wanted to be one of those creative people, I now I aspire to use the skills I am learning to work alongside others in building solutions for different communities around the world.
